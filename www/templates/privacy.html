When does BIZITOGO collect my personal information?

BIZITOGO gathers information from users in four ways: voluntary registration, cookies, web beacon technology and log files.

Voluntary Registration. When you register with BIZITOGO, we ask for certain necessary personal information. Other specific services may have additional registration requirements and may require you to provide additional information.

Cookies. When you access our websites, or use our applications and/or services and tools, we may place small data files on your computer or other device known as a “cookie”. BIZITOGO uses a browser cookie to remember your login name (but not your password), as a reminder to assist you the next time you log on to our Site. A cookie helps the Site to recall your specific information or subsequent visits in order to simplify the process of delivering relevant content and ease site navigation. You have the ability to accept or decline cookies by modifying your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer. If you choose to decline cookies, you may not be able to use interactive features of this or other websites that depend on cookies.

You may also encounter cookies from third parties, known as service providers, that we have allowed on our site that assist us with various aspects of our web site operations and services. BIZITOGO maintains no control over these third party cookies that you may encounter.

Web Beacons. BIZITOGO may use electronic images known as web beacons - sometimes called single-pixel gifs - that assist in delivering cookies on our sites and allow us to count users who have visited those pages and to deliver co-branded services. We may include web beacons in promotional email messages in order to determine whether messages have been opened and acted upon. BIZITOGO may also employ web beacons from third parties in order to help us compile aggregated statistics and determine the effectiveness of our promotional campaigns.

Log Files. BIZITOGO may also log standard information about your visit, including your IP address, browser type and language, access times, referring website addresses, the pages you view, the links you click and other actions taken in connection with the Site for the purposes of systems administration or to report information in aggregate form to our advertisers, sponsors, and corporate partners.
